2

Basic Structuring

WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?

The legal structure of your business determines how you are taxed, and the level of personal liability you may have. Choosing the wrong structure can lead to financial and legal complications.

WHAT DO WE PROVIDE?
UNDERSTANDING YOUR NEEDS
  • We begin with a detailed consultation to understand your business goals, projected revenue, and operational model.
EXPLAINING LEGAL STRUCTURES
  • LLC (Limited Liability Company): Offers flexibility and limits personal liability.
  • Partnership: Ideal for businesses with multiple founders sharing operational responsibilities.
  • Corporation: Best for businesses planning to raise capital or scale rapidly.
TAILORED RECOMMENDATIONS
  • Recommend a structure that aligns with your short-term needs and long-term vision.
  • Consider tax implications, operational flexibility, and investor preferences.
HOW DO WE HANDLE IT?

Our experts provide personalized guidance and manage the formalities of establishing the chosen structure, ensuring all legal requirements are met.

EMPLOYMENT REGULATIONS

4

Essential Agreements

WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?

Clear and well-drafted agreements protect your business and its stakeholders from disputes. They establish the rules, roles, and responsibilities of all parties involved, ensuring transparency and accountability.

WHAT DO WE PROVIDE?
Shareholder Agreements
  • Define the rights, obligations, and relationships of shareholders to avoid potential conflicts. Include provisions for ownership transfer, dividend distribution, and dispute resolution.
Operating Agreements
  • Detail the internal operations of the business, including management structure, decision-making processes, and profit-sharing models.
Founder’s Agreement
  • For multi-founder businesses, establish terms related to equity splits, responsibilities, and exit strategies.
Vendor & Client Contracts
  • Provide templates or draft simple agreements to formalize relationships with vendors or clients in your early stages.
HOW DO WE HANDLE IT?

We use clear, concise language to draft agreements that are legally sound yet easy to understand. We also ensure these documents comply with local legal standards and are tailored to your specific business model.
