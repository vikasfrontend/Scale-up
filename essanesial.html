The Essentials Package

FOR STARTUPS

Empowering early-stage businesses with a solid financial foundation. This package is specifically designed for entrepreneurs in the ideation or early operational stages. Recognizing the constraints of limited budgets, we focus on providing essential financial tools and actionable insights to help entrepreneurs establish a clear and reliable financial roadmap.

1

Basic Financial Modeling

WHY IT'S NEEDED

A startup's success depends heavily on understanding its financial landscape. Basic financial modeling offers clarity on how your business generates revenue, manages costs, and ensures profitability over time.

WHAT WE DELIVER
Cash Flow Projections
  • Month-by-month breakdown of expected cash inflows and outflows for the first 1–3 years.
  • Insight into liquidity management, helping you avoid cash shortages.
Profit & Loss Analysis
  • Detailed tracking of revenues, costs, and net profit to evaluate financial health.
  • Identifying break-even point to assess when your business will become profitable.
HOW DO WE HANDLE IT?
  • Use simple, yet effective tools (Excel-based or software-driven) to create a clear, easy-to-understand financial framework.
  • Ensure all assumptions (e.g., sales growth, operating expenses) are realistic and data-driven.
  • Customize the model to fit your business type (e.g., product-based, service-based, or SaaS).
